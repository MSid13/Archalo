Transparent insights into our multi-stage verification process and commitment to data quality.

Our Commitment to Accuracy

Archaeological data is inherently complex—regulations evolve, contact information changes, and local practices vary across jurisdictions. We recognize these challenges and have implemented a comprehensive verification process to ensure the highest level of accuracy possible.

Our verification methodology includes direct outreach to municipal and county offices, consultations with practicing archaeologists nationwide, and systematic cross-referencing of multiple authoritative sources. We maintain a rapid response protocol for corrections, investigating and addressing any reported discrepancies promptly.

Our Verification Process

A comprehensive approach to maintaining data integrity and reliability

Direct Municipal Verification

We establish direct contact with city and county offices to verify contact information, procedures, and regulatory requirements. Our team conducts regular follow-ups to maintain current information as protocols evolve.

Multi-Source Cross-Referencing

We employ a multi-stage verification process, cross-referencing official government sources, regulatory databases, and direct communications. This redundancy helps identify discrepancies and ensures data consistency.

Professional Consultations

We conduct ongoing consultations with practicing archaeologists nationwide to understand regional variations and local practices. These professional relationships provide valuable insights into jurisdiction-specific requirements and procedures.

Continuous Monitoring

We maintain an active monitoring system to track changes in regulations, contact information, and procedural requirements. Our update schedule prioritizes jurisdictions with frequent regulatory changes to ensure timely information.

Source Documentation

All information is traced to authoritative government sources and official documentation. We maintain detailed source records to ensure transparency and facilitate verification when regulatory language requires interpretation.

Community Feedback Integration

User-reported corrections are integral to our quality assurance process. We investigate all reported discrepancies promptly and implement verified corrections, ensuring our platform benefits from collective professional expertise.

Coverage & Expansion

We have established professional relationships with archaeologists and municipal offices across multiple regions, with particularly strong coverage on the East and West coasts. Our network continues to expand as we develop new connections and verify information across additional jurisdictions.

Building comprehensive, accurate coverage requires substantial effort—establishing relationships with local offices, conducting verification meetings, and validating information against multiple sources. We are committed to systematic expansion while maintaining our quality standards. Our coverage map is continuously growing to serve professionals nationwide.
